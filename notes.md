## Side Effects

#### Acute
<li> Low Blood Sugar (Hypoglycemia)
<li> Acidosis (CO2 < 15)
<li> Lack of energy
<li> Nausea / Vomiting


#### Long-term
<li> Constipation
<li> Diarrhea
<li> Kidney stones (7%)
<li> Low bone density (Osteopenia)
<li> Increased lipids (Hyperlipidemia)
<li> Low Growth / High Growth
<li> Pancreatitis (0.8%)

#### Managed Side Effects
<li> Miralax and/opr increased fluid intake constipation
<li> Medications for reflux

#### Supplements
<li> Calcium, fosferus, Potassium (multi-vitamin)

## Before Diet
<li> Increase water intake
<li> Sit down for family meals
<li> Decrease intake of high sugar foods (candy, cookies, and other sweets)
<li> Work on swallowing tablet form of medications
<li> Obtaining low carbohydrate products
